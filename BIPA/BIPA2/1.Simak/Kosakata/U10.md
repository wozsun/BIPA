# singkat

short (having little length or duration)
短暂的，简短的
Kalimat ini sangat singkat.
This sentence is very short.

brief (concise, to the point)
简洁的，简要的
Dia memberikan penjelasan yang singkat.
He gave a brief explanation.

# mengurutkan

to arrange (to put in a particular order)
排列，整理

He is arranging the books on the shelf.

to sort (to organize items into categories)
分类，整理
Komputer ini dapat mengurutkan data dengan cepat.
This computer can sort data quickly.

# alur

groove (a long, narrow cut or depression in a surface)

He made a groove on the wood with that tool.

course (a path or direction followed by something)
路线，方向
Kapal itu mengikuti alur laut yang aman.
The ship followed a safe sea course.
